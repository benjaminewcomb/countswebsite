Re-use blackwater/ toilet-to-tap

Population increased 38% from 2004 to 2023 in the St. John's water managment district which includes Volusia County.

Per the US Drought Monitor the majority of Florida is in an 'extreme drought'.

These two facts mean our aquifers are being depleted.

Aquifers are important to have storage of drinking water.

Options to address this problem include using less water - possibly with increased xeriscaping, desalination, or re-use blackwater; potentially all three.
