About Counts

Greetings all & good tidings to you & yours. Soon, a seat on the City Commission for district five is up for consideration, & by your leave & good grace, I intend to answer the call to civic service. Born here, at Halifax Hospital, I mostly grew up living, at times, between Daytona, Favoretta & Bunnell. My family growing up had access to somewhat meager means, though thankfully never quite the worse & we survived. I attended high school briefly in Flagler, then here at Mainland, then moved for my last year in school to Tennessee, where I graduated & joined the Navy as a Nuclear Field Electrician's Mate for six years, having had the privilege of serving as part of the crew of USS Louisville, SSN 724 - a Los Angeles class nuclear powered Fast Attack Submarine home-ported out of Pearl Harbor, Hawaii & making port in many wonderful cities around the Pacific. Leaving the Navy in 2017, I have since lived, worked in, or had occasion to visit for work, several states including Tennessee, Georgia, Montana, Nebraska, California & South Carolina. Never while living or visiting those beautiful states, however, did I quite feel at home; I am a Florida-man through & through & so I have returned home to go to college & God willing, I will be able to afford to live in the land my heart pines for every day I spend away - thank you kindly.
